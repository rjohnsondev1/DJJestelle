Meet J. Estelle, the vibrant, versatile and dynamic DJ who is sure to leave you mesmerized with her electrifying beats. Formerly known as DJ Senseless, J. Estelle's journey into the world of music began in Union, NJ, where she was born and raised. J. Estelle is a highly sought-after DJ, music producer, and artist. J. Estelle has graced some of the most prestigious stages with her exceptional talent. From the 125th Annual Valor Awards to the Garden State Equality Ball, from Union Pride to the Soul House Festival, J. Estelle crafts unforgettable experiences with her music bringing audiences to their feet. J. Estelle's magnetic presence has also lit up events and festivals like the Newark Kids Rock Jersey Club Festival, NYC Aids Walk, Movies Under the Stars, and Girls Night Out - Union. In 2017, she achieved a remarkable milestone, winning the first-ever Battle of the DJs, solidifying her position as a force to be reckoned with in the indie DJ scene. Her dedication and artistry were further recognized in 2019 when she was proudly inducted into the esteemed Brick Bandits Jersey Club music group. Not only has J. Estelle conquered the live event circuit, but she has also graced the airwaves of renowned platforms like The Plug Radio and Tribal Expressions internet radio show, where she skillfully weaves together an array of house music sub-genres, leaving listeners craving for more. Whether she's igniting the dance floor at birthday and graduation parties, adding flair to family reunions, mesmerizing audiences at showcases, or setting the perfect tone for corporate events, J. Estelle's infectious energy and passion for music never fail to create an unforgettable experience for everyone present. Electrifying beats, masterful mixes, and an unwavering passion for music and service converge into an unforgettable experience that will leave you wanting more.
